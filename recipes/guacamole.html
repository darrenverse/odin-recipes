Guacamole




Description


Delicious guacamole that will be an absoulute treat for yourself. It is easy to make and uses fresh ingredients.

This guacomole can also be used for parties, dinners and get togethers. Using tomatoes and onions plus touches of lime and salt.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados- peeled,pitted & mashed
  • 1 lime , juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma ( plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayeene pepper ( optional )

Directions

  1. Mashed avocados, lime juice & salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro & garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve Immedicately, or cover & refridgerate for 1 hour for improved flavor
