Taco Dip




Description


Delicious seven layers of taco dip that you and your cronies will love.

You wont be able to resist this dip that has onions and other delicious ingredients that everyone will start asking for the recipe.

Ingedients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
  • 1 ( 1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 1 ( 16 ounce ) container sour cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 ( 16 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 bunch chopped green onions
  • 1 small head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 ( 6 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained

Steps

  1. Blend refried beans & taco seasoning in a medium bowl; spread mixture ontp a large serving platter.
  2. Mix sourcream & cream cheese in a medium bowl; spread over refried beans.
  3. Top with salsa. Layer tomato, belpepper, green onions & lettuce over top.
  4. Sprinkle over cheddar cheese & garnish with black olives
