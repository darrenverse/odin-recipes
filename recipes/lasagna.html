Lasagna




Description


Delicious lasagna Barefoot contessa inspired. If this website is working you should be seeing a picture of jalepno poppers wrapped in bacon and that is okay. It is a lasagna with four different types of cheese and simple noodles and sauce. Making sauce from scratch can be time consuming so canned sauce is also delcious. Make sure to season the meat when cooking prior to assembling and throwing into the oven.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • marinara sauce
  • ground turkey or beef
  • parmesan cheese
  • mozarella cheese
  • ricotta cheese
  • goat cheese
